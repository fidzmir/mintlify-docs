M-TPM Portal is a mobile Android application built for production and manufacturing teams that need a reliable, always-on-hand tool for Total Productive Maintenance (TPM) management. Whether you're walking the production floor, responding to an equipment alert, or auditing asset labels during a shift handover, M-TPM Portal puts every maintenance workflow in your pocket — replacing clipboards, disconnected spreadsheets, and manual inspection rounds with a single, structured mobile portal.

## The Problems M-TPM Portal Solves

Modern production facilities run into the same friction points again and again: maintenance logs stored in siloed systems (or on paper), inspection cycles that slip through the cracks when shifts change, and label auditing processes that rely entirely on human memory and eyesight. M-TPM Portal was designed to eliminate each of these pain points head-on.

- **Disconnected maintenance logs** — All maintenance events are captured in a structured, timestamped digital log, accessible to every authorized team member in real time.
- **Missed inspections** — Active asset and tag control gives supervisors a live view of which assets have been checked, flagged, or are overdue for attention.
- **Manual label auditing** — AI-powered OCR, driven by Google's Gemini API, reads physical asset labels through your device camera and cross-references them against your records automatically.

## AI-Powered Label Auditing

M-TPM Portal includes an AI auditing capability powered by Google's Gemini API. Using your Android device's camera, the app captures images of physical asset labels and uses Gemini's multimodal OCR to extract and validate label data — part numbers, serial numbers, maintenance codes, and more — against your active asset records. This eliminates transcription errors, accelerates audits, and creates a verifiable digital record of every label scan.

<Note>
  AI auditing features — including label scanning and OCR validation — require a valid Gemini API key. You will be prompted to enter your key in the app's Settings screen before AI features become available. See the [Quickstart](/quickstart) for setup instructions.
</Note>

## Who Uses M-TPM Portal

M-TPM Portal is designed for the people closest to production assets and maintenance workflows:

- **Maintenance Technicians** — Log work orders, record completed maintenance, and scan asset tags during routine rounds.
- **Production Supervisors** — Monitor asset health, review open maintenance tickets, and verify that inspections are being completed on schedule.
- **Quality Engineers** — Audit asset labels for accuracy and compliance, and pull maintenance history reports to support quality investigations.

M-TPM Portal is a mobile Android application that puts production maintenance management in the palm of your hand. Log maintenance events on the shop floor, scan asset tags, review maintenance history, and let AI-powered OCR auditing catch label discrepancies — all from a single app.

## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the App">
    Download and install M-TPM Portal on your Android device or emulator. See the [Installation guide](/installation) for details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Your API Key">
    Set up your Gemini API key to enable AI-powered OCR auditing features. See [API Key Setup](/configuration/api-key).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Logging Maintenance">
    Open the app, tap the **+** button on the dashboard, and submit your first maintenance record. See [Logging a Maintenance Event](/maintenance/log-entry).
  </Step>
</Steps>

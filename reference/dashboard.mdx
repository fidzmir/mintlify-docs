The Dashboard is your home screen and command center inside M-TPM Portal. Every time you open the app, you land here — giving you an at-a-glance view of your maintenance operation's health, quick access to the most common actions, and a live feed of recent activity across all assets. Whether you're starting a morning floor walk, responding to a breakdown, or checking overdue events before a shift handover, the Dashboard is the first place you'll look.

## Layout Overview

The Dashboard is organized into four distinct areas stacked vertically:

1. **Top Navigation Bar** — App title, user avatar, and icon shortcuts to other screens.
2. **Status Summary Cards** — Four KPI cards showing real-time counts of maintenance event states.
3. **Quick-Action Buttons** — One-tap shortcuts to the most frequent workflows.
4. **Recent Activity Feed** — A chronological list of the last 10 maintenance events.

---

## Status Summary Cards

The status cards update in real time as records are created, edited, or resolved. Tap any card to open a filtered list view showing only events in that state.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Open Records" icon="folder-open">
    The total count of maintenance events that have been created but not yet resolved. An event is considered "open" if its status is `pending` or `in_progress`. A rising number here is a signal to triage your backlog.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In Progress" icon="spinner">
    The number of maintenance tasks that are actively being worked on — records whose status has been set to `in_progress`. Use this to monitor how much work your team currently has on their plate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Overdue" icon="clock">
    Events that have passed their `estimated_completion` datetime without being marked `completed`. Overdue events are highlighted in red throughout the app. Tap this card to see the full overdue list and take immediate action.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Completed Today" icon="circle-check">
    A count of events marked `completed` within the last 24 hours. This card resets at midnight local time and is a quick way to gauge daily throughput.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Quick-Action Buttons

Four prominent buttons sit between the status cards and the activity feed, covering the actions you perform most often.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="+ New Record" icon="plus">
    Opens a blank [Maintenance Log Form](/reference/maintenance-form) so you can manually enter a new maintenance event. Use this when you need to log work that wasn't triggered by a tag scan.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scan Tag" icon="qrcode">
    Launches the [Tag Scanner](/reference/tag-scanner) screen with the camera ready to read an asset label. After a successful scan, you can view asset details or log an event directly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Start AI Audit" icon="robot">
    Opens the AI-powered OCR audit workflow powered by Google's Gemini API. Point your camera at any asset label and M-TPM Portal will read, parse, and validate the label's data against your records.
  </Card>
  <Card title="View History" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    Takes you to the full [Reports](/reference/reports) screen with the **Maintenance Summary** report pre-selected, so you can quickly review historical activity.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Recent Activity Feed

The Recent Activity Feed displays the **last 10 maintenance events** logged in the system, sorted with the most recent at the top. Each row in the feed shows:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Asset ID** | The identifier of the asset this event belongs to. |
| **Event Type** | Icon and label indicating `corrective`, `preventive`, or `inspection`. |
| **Status Badge** | Color-coded pill: grey (`pending`), blue (`in_progress`), green (`completed`), red (`overdue`). |
| **Technician** | Name or ID of the assigned technician, if set. |
| **Timestamp** | Relative time of the last update (e.g., "3 min ago"), with the absolute datetime on long-press. |

Tap any row in the feed to open the full record in the [Maintenance Log Form](/reference/maintenance-form) in read or edit mode, depending on the event's current status.

## Navigation Bar

The bottom navigation bar is persistent across all screens and gives you access to every major section of M-TPM Portal.

| Icon | Label | Destination |
|---|---|---|
| 🏠 House | **Dashboard** | Returns you to this screen from anywhere in the app. |
| 📋 Clipboard | **Records** | Full list of all maintenance records with search and filter controls. |
| 📷 Camera | **Scan** | Shortcut directly to the [Tag Scanner](/reference/tag-scanner) screen. |
| 📊 Chart bar | **Reports** | Opens the [Reports](/reference/reports) screen for generating and exporting summaries. |
| ⚙️ Gear | **Settings** | App configuration: user profile, notification preferences, API settings, and data sync options. |

The currently active section is highlighted with your organization's accent color. The **Scan** icon in the center of the bar is slightly elevated and larger than the others, making it immediately reachable during a fast-paced floor walk.
